About Me

My name is Gomolemo Tshenye, and I am currently a second-year Bachelor of Computer Science student at Varsity College in Pretoria. I previously earned a Higher Certificate in Web and Mobile Application Development, which laid a solid foundation in both front-end and back-end development principles. Through my academic journey, I have gained hands-on experience with a diverse set of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, Java, and Python. I am also proficient in working with cloud computing services, particularly Microsoft Azure, where I have deployed and maintained web-based applications.

My portfolio includes a variety of projects that reflect my technical versatility and problem-solving capabilities. Notable examples include the development of a cybersecurity awareness chatbot designed to educate users about common online threats, the creation of mobile-responsive websites optimized for performance across devices, and the implementation of cloud-enabled applications using ASP.NET Core MVC and Azure services. These projects have not only strengthened my software engineering skills but also deepened my understanding of secure coding practices, database integration, and agile methodologies.

In addition to my technical work, I have gained practical experience in administrative and customer-facing roles. As a registration assistant, I supported student enrollment activities, managed event logistics, and interacted with prospective students and parents—enhancing my communication, organization, and teamwork abilities. These soft skills have proven invaluable in both academic and project collaboration settings.

I have a strong passion for user interface and user experience (UI/UX) design, as I believe intuitive and accessible software is key to user satisfaction. I actively use version control tools like Git and GitHub to manage codebases and collaborate effectively with teams. I am committed to continuous learning and regularly explore emerging technologies, frameworks, and best practices to stay current in this fast-evolving industry. My long-term goal is to contribute meaningfully to software solutions that improve everyday life while advancing my career as a full-stack developer or cloud solutions architect.
