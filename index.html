what I do

A working developer, not a poster of one.

I'm in my third year of IIE Bachelor of Computer and Information Sciences in Application Development at the IIE(Emeris) in South Africa, currently shipping projects across the stack — Android in Kotlin, web APIs in .NET, React prototypes, and a statistical engine of my own. Below: three of the things I spend the most time on.

01 — build

Full-stack systems

Production-shaped .NET solutions with JWT auth, MVC frontends, NUnit test projects, and full Docker orchestration. I like architectures that survive a teardown review.

02 — ship

Android & cloud

Kotlin apps backed by Firebase Auth, Firestore sync, and Room offline storage. Hand-drawn Canvas charts when the off-the-shelf option is wrong for the data.

03 — think

Data & prediction

My own statistical analysis engine for the South African lottery — five prediction models and a walk-forward backtest. Probably the most fun I've had with arrays.
